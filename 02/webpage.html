Nikki Hahn

I like to travel--it can be for a day or for longer. Lately water has been calming for me, it puts me in a happy place. I really connect with areas where I can see the water flow. It could be by taking a walk in Canton to Harbor East or it could be walking along the canals in Venice or Amsterdam.

Since I have been enjoying water these days, I really want to learn how to kayak. It seems like a good slow-paced activity. I can get a good workout, but enjoy the scenery along the way. I just hope I don't spend my whole time learning how not to topple over!

Cooking is another interest of mine. I like to combine the flavors of where I have recently traveled and add them to my arsenal of recipes. My latest interest is with lemon zest. It flavor many different dishes I had in Sorrento and Positano. Amazing that a seemingly ordinary, everyday fruit can add so much to a dish.
